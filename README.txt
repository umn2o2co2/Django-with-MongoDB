About our Project:
Only Stupid Doubts(OSD) is a platform where students can freely ask doubts and get help from their peers as well as experts.
This will help them in academic development and getting timely response to their questions.

Features:
1)Ask Questions: From Maths to Algorithms, whatever your doubt maybe…share with us on OSD!  
2)Discuss Answers: Get a wide range of answers for all your queries from any of our users!
3)Get Teacher’s Assistance: Get Answers and Detailed Explanations from our Experts
